MINECRAFT


Your Massive Workspace! Draw and Create your own MineCraft Designs! Click On The Following Keys to Draw Blocks and use Commands!!!


  1. Click On T for A Tree's Trunk!
  2. Click On D for A Dark Green Wool Block!
  3. Click on L for A Light Green Wool Block!
  4. Click on G for a Ground Block or A Dirt Block!
  5. Click on W for A Wall Block!
  6. Click on Y for A Yellow Color Wall Block!
  7. Click on R for A Roof Block!
  8. Click on C for a Cloud Block!
  9. Click on U to Draw A Unique Special Mysterious Block!
  10. Click On Shift And Up Arrow to Increase Starting Size and Click on Shift And Down Arrow To Decrease Starting Size!
  11. Click On Shift And Left Arrow to Decrease Starting X AND Y and Click on Shift And Right Arrow To Increase Starting X AND Y!
  12. Use Arrow Keys To Move Player!
  13. Drag The blocks out of the Workspace to delete them!
  14. Drag a certain area to create a Group!
  15. Build and Enjoy!
